Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Introduction

Beckllen Creative ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us about our services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Form Data: Your name, email address, business name, and any message content you provide.
  • Email Communications: Content of direct email communications you send us.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Technical information including IP address, browser type, and pages visited — used for analytics only.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies to improve your experience. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies.

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate about potential projects
  • Send follow-up information related to your contact request
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Web3Forms: Processes contact form submissions. See web3forms.com/privacy.
  • Sanity.io: Powers our blog CMS. See sanity.io/privacy.
  • Google Fonts: Serves typefaces. See policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Cloudflare: May proxy our site for performance. See cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and email communications for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us directly.

Your Rights

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of any marketing communications

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
